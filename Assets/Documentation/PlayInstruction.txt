Play Instruction

1. Press Enter Key to start playing this game. When you hit Enter Key, music starts playing and fireballs(Monster's attack) start flying to the player.

2. Press Up, Right, Left, and Down Arrow keys when fireballs hit buttons in front of the player. 

3. Beat the opponent monsters before you get beaten!

* Click Quit button to exit the game.
* Click Menu button to go to menu scene.
